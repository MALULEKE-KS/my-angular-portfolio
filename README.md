## Platforms in Development

### FundsLink Academy
National education funding platform connecting students with bursaries, NSFAS, SETA funds, and scholarships.

- **Problem:** 342,000+ students excluded yearly due to fragmented applications
- **Stack:** Next.js → FastAPI AI Service → PostgreSQL
- **Status:** Active development

### Maphophe Community System
Digital governance platform for rural villages — announcements, service requests, community voting.

- **Problem:** Rural villages lack digital infrastructure
- **Stack:** Next.js → PostgreSQL
- **Status:** Design complete

### KSDRILL Reserve Bank
Micro-savings platform with goal-setting and automated deposits.

- **Problem:** Limited access to microfinance tools
- **Stack:** Next.js → PostgreSQL
- **Status:** Design complete
